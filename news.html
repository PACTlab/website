Updates, events, and highlights from the lab.

March 2026

PACT Lab officially opens at the University of Pittsburgh

Dr. Hauser and the PACT Lab are now up and running in the Department of Communication Science and Disorders at Pitt. The lab focuses on precision diagnostics for sensorineural hearing loss.

February 2026

New paper on multimetric biomarker classification of SNHL published

Our latest work demonstrates improved classification accuracy for cochlear pathology subtypes using a non-invasive physiological biomarker battery across chinchilla and human subjects.

Participant recruitment open for our aging and hearing study

We are currently recruiting adults aged 45–75 for a study examining how the inner ear changes with age and noise exposure. Learn more →
