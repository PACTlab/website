About the lab

Precision Audiology & Clinical Translation Lab

The PACT Lab investigates how diverse cochlear pathologies contribute to individual differences in auditory perception and hearing outcomes. Using a cross-species framework spanning human participants and preclinical animal models, we develop and validate physiological biomarker batteries that go beyond the audiogram — grounding diagnosis in pathophysiology rather than audibility alone.
